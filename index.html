What is Drawing Board?

Drawing Board is a full sized collapsable whiteboard that fits in your bag!

Why carry a whiteboard around?

While there are many drawing apps and other options, a whiteboard succeeds with simplicity and size. Drawing with an app requires a tablet (smartphones just arent big enough) and a stylus, because drawing with a finger is messier and slower than you'd think. Nothing is simpler than writing and drawing by hand on a large whiteboard. Until now, the whiteboard was tethered to the wall as it just wasn't feasible to carry around a decent size board. Drawing Board is the answer and allows the power of the whiteboard to be taken anywhere. Diagramming ideas is now cheaper, easier, and more portable than ever!

When will Drawing Board be ready?

Drawing Board already has a functional prototype that works extremely well. However, there are production issues that must be resolved before we can move forward. There is no estimate on when this will be available currently other than as soon as possible!
